RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is entered into on this 1st day of January, 2024, between METROPOLITAN PROPERTIES LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of California ("Landlord"), and JOHN DOE and JANE DOE ("Tenant").

PREMISES
Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the residential property located at 123 Maple Street, Apartment 4B, Los Angeles, CA 90210 ("Premises"), consisting of a two-bedroom, one-bathroom apartment.

TERM
The initial term of this lease shall commence on January 1, 2024, and shall expire on December 31, 2024, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof. This lease shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS
Tenant shall pay to Landlord monthly rent in the amount of Two Thousand Four Hundred Dollars ($2,400.00), due and payable in advance on the first (1st) day of each calendar month. Rent payments received after the fifth (5th) day of the month shall incur a late fee of Fifty Dollars ($50.00) per day until paid in full. All payments shall be made in legal tender of the United States.

SECURITY DEPOSIT
Upon execution of this Agreement, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of Four Thousand Eight Hundred Dollars ($4,800.00) as security for Tenant's faithful performance of all terms and conditions hereof. Said deposit shall be returned to Tenant within thirty (30) days after termination of this lease, less any deductions for damages, unpaid rent, or other charges as permitted by law.

UTILITIES AND SERVICES
Tenant shall be responsible for and shall promptly pay all charges for electricity, gas, water, sewer, telephone, internet, cable television, and all other utilities and services supplied to or used in connection with the Premises, except for building maintenance and landscaping services, which shall be provided by Landlord.

USE AND OCCUPANCY
The Premises shall be used and occupied solely as a private residential dwelling by Tenant and Tenant's immediate family members listed herein. No other persons shall occupy the Premises without prior written consent of Landlord. Tenant shall not use or permit the Premises to be used for any illegal purposes or any purposes that will increase the rate of insurance on the building or cause cancellation of any insurance policy.

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Landlord shall maintain the structural elements of the building, including the roof, foundation, and exterior walls, in good repair. Tenant shall maintain the interior of the Premises in clean and sanitary condition and shall be responsible for all repairs and maintenance arising from Tenant's use, misuse, or negligence. Tenant shall be liable for all damages to the Premises caused by Tenant, Tenant's family, guests, or invitees, regardless of intent or negligence.

ALTERATIONS
Tenant shall make no alterations, additions, or improvements to the Premises without the prior written consent of Landlord. Any alterations, additions, or improvements made by Tenant shall become the property of Landlord upon termination of this lease, unless Landlord elects to require Tenant to restore the Premises to its original condition.

PETS
No pets, animals, or birds of any kind shall be kept in or about the Premises without the prior written consent of Landlord. If Landlord consents to pets, Tenant shall pay an additional monthly pet fee of One Hundred Dollars ($100.00) and an additional pet deposit of Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00).

DEFAULT AND TERMINATION
If Tenant fails to pay rent when due, violates any provision of this Agreement, or abandons the Premises, Landlord may, at Landlord's option and without prejudice to any other rights or remedies, terminate this lease by giving Tenant three (3) days written notice to cure such default or quit the Premises. If Tenant fails to cure the default within said period, this lease shall terminate and Landlord may pursue all available legal remedies.

ATTORNEY'S FEES
In the event of any legal action arising out of this Agreement, the prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney's fees and costs from the non-prevailing party.

LIABILITY LIMITATION
Tenant acknowledges and agrees that Landlord shall not be liable for any damage or injury to Tenant, Tenant's family, guests, or invitees, or to any property, occurring on or about the Premises, unless such damage or injury is caused by the gross negligence or willful misconduct of Landlord. Tenant agrees to indemnify and hold Landlord harmless from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from Tenant's use of the Premises.

ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and may not be modified except by written agreement signed by both parties. This Agreement shall be binding upon the heirs, successors, and assigns of the parties.
